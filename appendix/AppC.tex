\section{$N$-jettiness subtraction ingredients}
\label{ap:N-jet-ing}


Here we present the components used to build the subtraction ingredients
of Ref.~\cite{Gaunt:2015pea}. Legs are labelled $a$, $b$ (incoming) and
$c$ (outgoing jet), and the variable $j$ is the flavour that the PDFs are summed over.

\begin{align}

corresponding to $h_{10}\cdot h_{11}$, is subtracted before the
comparison is made (Fig.~\ref{fig:mathematica-C20-sub}). The initial
comparison in Fig.~\ref{fig:mathematica-h21compare} leaves a residual
in $\gamma^{(1)}_\ell$, which does not vanish symbolically since the
SCET and \textsc{ARES} conventions for $\gamma^{(1)}$ differ. Substituting explicit values for the
$q\bar{q}\to Zg$ channel, the residual vanishes exactly. The other channels follow in
the same way.
