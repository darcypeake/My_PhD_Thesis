\section{$N$-jettiness subtraction ingredients}
\label{ap:N-jet-ing}
Here we present the components used to build the subtraction ingredients
of Ref.~\cite{Gaunt:2015pea}. Legs are labelled $a$, $b$ (incoming) and
$c$ (outgoing jet), and the variable $k$ is the flavour that the PDFs are summed over.
We have suppressed the arguments for brevity.

\begin{subequations}
\label{eq:C11-ingred}
\begin{align}
J_{c,1}^{(1)} &= 4C_c\,, \\
\mathcal{I}_{ak,1}^{(1)} &= 4C_a\,\delta_{ak}\,\delta(1-z)\,, \\

\mathcal{I}_{bk,0}^{(1)} &= -2\gamma_b^{(0)}\,\delta_{bk}\,\delta(1-z)+2P_{bk}\,, \\

    4C_a\!\left(\ln\frac{Q_{ab}^2}{Q_a Q_b}+\ln\frac{Q_{ac}^2}{Q_a Q_c}

\end{subequations}
\begin{subequations}
